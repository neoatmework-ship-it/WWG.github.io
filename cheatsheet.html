Gallery Secrets Guide

There are currently 13 hidden web pages completely disconnected from the main gallery grid. Here is exactly how to find every single one of them.

🔍 Search Bar Secrets

Go to the main gallery page (index.html) and type these exact words into the search bar, then hit Enter.

1. The Void
🌑
Trigger: Type void into the gallery search bar and press Enter.
What it is: An infinite falling sequence with floating, cryptic messages implying you have slipped out of the confines of the gallery.
2. Ghost.exe
👻
Trigger: Type ghost into the gallery search bar and press Enter.
What it is: A haunted, 1980s terminal where you can type questions and have a genuinely unsettling AI conversation with "Entity E-1983".
3. Classified Document
📄
Trigger: Type classified into the gallery search bar and press Enter.
What it is: A heavily redacted internal government document detailing the containment procedures for the very gallery you are browsing.
🎬 Action-Triggered Secrets

You must perform specific, hidden actions inside the existing gallery websites to be teleported to these pages.

4. Mirror Gallery
🪞
Website: Main Gallery (index.html)
Trigger: Click the large gradient "WWG" title at the top of the page 5 times in quick succession.
What it is: A visually inverted clone of the gallery. Everything is backwards, and the previously hidden secret pages are listed but mockingly inaccessible.
5. The Backrooms
🏨
Website: Infinite Maze
Trigger: Stubbornly run your player square directly into solid walls 20 times. A flickering door will appear at the bottom.
What it is: An endless, humming labyrinth of damp yellow carpet and mono-yellow wallpaper. A pure liminal horror experience.
6. 103.7 FM Broadcast
📻
Website: Signal Interceptor
Trigger: Carefully drag the frequency slider to exactly 103.7 MHz and leave it completely still for 3 seconds until a "LOCK ACQUIRED" button appears.
What it is: A simulated pirate radio station broadcasting on a deserted frequency, warning you about the very sites you are exploring.
7. The Underground
🎩
Website: Memory Matrix
Trigger: Successfully play the memory game and survive until you reach Level 10. A glowing velvet door will spawn.
What it is: A dark, sophisticated speakeasy website that acts as a haven for those who have "remembered enough."
8. Department of Waiting (Limbo)
⏳
Website: Neon Clock
Trigger: Click directly on the face of the glowing circle clock 7 times quickly.
What it is: A bureaucratic nightmare dimension where you are given a ticket number and forced to wait as the display slowly, uselessly updates.
9. The Chosen
⭐
Website: Emoji Slots
Trigger: Keep pulling the lever until you legitimately hit the 7️⃣7️⃣7️⃣ jackpot (a 1-in-3,375 chance).
What it is: A dramatic, gold-plated ceremonial decree claiming you have been initiated into the Order of the Triple Seven.
10. Static Breakthrough
📺
Website: Signal Interceptor
Trigger: Drag the Noise slider all the way up to 100% and leave it there for 5 full seconds.
What it is: A generative, deafening field of TV static that slowly overlays cryptic, faded text.
11. The Deep
🌊
Website: Terrain Generator
Trigger: Hold the S key on your keyboard and continuously scroll down with your mouse wheel until you descend out of bounds.
What it is: An ominous, dark green underwater void where particles float upwards, prompting you to "ascend".
12. The Corner Room
💽
Website: DVD Simulator
Trigger: Adjust speed and size until the DVD logo hits exactly into the corner of the screen 1 time.
What it is: A room celebrating the ultimate satisfaction of the perfect corner bounce.
13. The Useful Button
🟢
Website: Useless Buttons
Trigger: Type the classic Konami code anywhere on the page: Up, Up, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right, B, A.
What it is: Finally, a button that actually does something productive (sort of).
